Subregular Workshop!

Mon 15 April 2019
by Ayla Karakas
in 2019-Spring

Hi all! The week of Subregular Workshop is upon us. From April 18-20 the Computational Phonology Workshop will be held, overlapping with our weekly meeting. Topics include fixed point logics, subregular formal languages, transductions with logic and automata, algorithmic learning, and phonological phenomena (harmony, stress, optionality, and more). It is more of an informal forum than organized talks, and open to everyone! A schedule can be found here.

Hope to see you there!
