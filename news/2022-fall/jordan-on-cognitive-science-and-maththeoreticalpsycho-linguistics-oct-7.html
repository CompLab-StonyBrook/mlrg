Jordan on Cognitive Science and Math/Theoretical/Psycho Linguistics (Oct 7)

Mon 03 October 2022
by Zhengxiang (Jack) Wang
in 2022-Fall

This Friday (Oct 7), Jordan will be discussing David Marr's levels of analysis in cognitive science, and where and how mathematical linguistics fits into our understanding of linguistic cognition and what that tells us about theoretical linguistics and psycholinguistics. Chapters from Marr (1982) and Lignos (2013) will also be discussed.

You can access both materials by clicking here using your Stony Brook Google account.
